WHAT'S FOR LUNCH BTOWN?

There are hundreds of restaurants in the Bloomington area, with a large portion being unique, locally owned eateries. For a visitor or even a townie it can be hard to decide what to eat for lunch. With "WHAT'S FOR LUNCH BTOWN" we have aggregated a list of places to eat for lunch in Bloomington, Indiana into a fun web app to help you choose a new lunchtime destination.
